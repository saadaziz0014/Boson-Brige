Our Portfolio

Take a closer look at some of our projects, highlighting our expertise and creative capabilities.

Gear Grit

Gear Grit is a Unity 3d game bicycle stunt based leveler game. It features a unique take at traditional bicycle controls while also featuring an open world mode.

Survive

Survive is an opne world FPS survival game made using Unity. The player has to survive the elements as well as cannibals and wild boars.

Spidey's Quest

Spidey's Quest is an exciting open-world adventure game developed for mobile, where Spidey, a brave spider is on a mission to rescue his abducted wife

Crime City

Crime City is an action-packed open-world mobile game where players take on the role of a gangster entering a new city to rise to power.

You Are Dead

You Are Dead is a 3rd person shooter game developed using Unity, designed for PC to deliver a thrilling, high-energy experience.

Pluck It

Pluckit is developed using Unity. The game brings a unique twist to the classic Ping Pong format, combining fast-paced gameplay with a fun competitive edge.

Cook'em

Cook 'Em is an engaging and fun-filled cooking simulation game where players take on the role of a chef managing their own kitchen.

Sphere Serpent

Sphere Serpent is a unique and thrilling game developed for PC, where players control a serpent-like creature that twists around a globe.

Foot Pong

Foot Pong is an exciting and fast-paced arcade game developed in Unity, combining the classic mechanics of Pong with the thrill of football.

Bird Smashers

Bird Smashers is developed using Unity. It blends vibrant visuals with addictive gameplay to create a fun experience for players of all ages.
