Peezious

Project Description

Peezious is a sleek and efficient food delivery app built with Java, designed to simplify and enhance the food ordering experience. Users can explore a wide range of restaurants, browse detailed menus, and place orders with ease. With robust backend functionality and a seamless interface, Peezious ensures fast delivery tracking, secure payments, and personalized recommendations. Whether you're craving comfort food or trying out something new, Peezious combines speed, reliability, and convenience to bring delicious meals right to your doorstep.

Key Features

  • Explore a curated list of food items offered by Peezious
  • User-friendly interface with seamless navigation for effortless food ordering.
  • Real-time order tracking to monitor your delivery from kitchen to doorstep.
  • Secure payment options, including card, wallet, and cash on delivery.
  • Personalized recommendations based on user preferences and past orders.
  • Easy-to-use search filters to search by name, cusine, or favorites.
  • Reliable customer support for smooth resolution of queries and concerns.

Project Duration

2 month (June 2024 - August 2024)

Additional Information

Peezious is made using Android Studio and Java while using firebase for real time database connectivity.
