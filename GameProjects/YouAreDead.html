You Are Dead

Project Description

You Are Dead is an intense shooting and fighting game developed exclusively for PC, where players engage in combat against waves of enemies in a variety of environments. Set in a post-apocalyptic world, the game combines fast-paced action with strategic combat mechanics. Players must use an array of weapons and fight their way through dangerous foes to survive.

Key Features

  • Fast-paced shooting and hand-to-hand combat mechanics for immersive gameplay.
  • Wide range of weapons, from guns to melee tools, for versatile combat strategies.
  • Challenging enemy waves with unique AI behaviors and difficulty levels.
  • Intense, story-driven missions set in a post-apocalyptic world.
  • Upgradeable skills and weapons to enhance player power and tactics.
  • Visually stunning environments with high-quality graphics and dynamic lighting.
  • Leaderboards and achievements to track player progress and encourage competition.
  • PC-exclusive performance optimizations for smooth and immersive gameplay.

Project Duration

1 month (August 2023 - September 2023)

Additional Information

You Are Dead is developed using Unity, designed for PC to deliver a thrilling, high-energy experience. The game offers deep combat mechanics, strategic weapon use, and a sandbox of madness.Regular updates will introduce new missions, enemies, and weapons to keep the game fresh and challenging.
