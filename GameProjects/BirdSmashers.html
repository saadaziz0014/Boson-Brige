Bird Smashers

Project Description

Bird Smashers is a fun and physics-based arcade game developed in Unity, inspired by the mechanics of Angry Birds. Players launch projectiles to knock down structures and defeat mischievous birds causing chaos. With challenging levels, unique power-ups, and creative designs, Bird Smashers combines strategy and precision for an exciting gaming experience.

Key Features

  • Physics-based gameplay with destructible structures and obstacles.
  • Variety of projectiles, each with unique abilities and effects.
  • Challenging levels with increasing difficulty and creative designs.
  • Colorful and engaging visuals with dynamic animations.
  • Power-ups and bonuses to help players tackle tough levels.
  • Reward system with stars and achievements to unlock additional content.
  • Offline play support for fun on the go.

Project Duration

1 month (June 2023 - July 2023)

Additional Information

Bird Smashers is developed using Unity. It blends vibrant visuals with addictive gameplay to create a fun experience for players of all ages. Future updates will include new levels, projectile types, and seasonal themes to keep the game fresh and engaging.
