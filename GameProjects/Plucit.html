Pluck It

Project Description

Pluckit is an exciting, fast-paced game developed in Unity, combining the classic mechanics of Ping Pong with a twistâ€”players use paddles to strike a puck instead of a ball. The objective is to keep the puck in play while trying to score points by landing it in the opponent's goal. With simple controls and competitive gameplay, Pluckit offers endless fun for both casual players and competitive enthusiasts.

Key Features

  • Classic Ping Pong gameplay with a puck instead of a ball.
  • Multiplayer mode for head-to-head matches with friends or AI.
  • Fast-paced action with dynamic puck physics for unpredictable gameplay.
  • Customizable paddles and arenas to create a personalized experience.
  • Power-ups and special abilities that add strategy and excitement to matches.
  • Vibrant graphics and smooth animations for an immersive gaming experience.
  • Leaderboards and achievements to encourage competition and replayability.

Project Duration

1 month (June 2024 - July 2024)

Additional Information

Pluckit is developed using Unity. The game brings a unique twist to the classic Ping Pong format, combining fast-paced gameplay with a fun competitive edge. Regular updates are planned to introduce new arenas, power-ups, and challenges to keep the game fresh and exciting.
