Survive

Project Description

Survive is an immersive open-world shooting and hunting game developed in Unity, set in the heart of a dense jungle. Players explore vast, realistic environments while battling wild animals, hunting for resources, and surviving against the elements. With intense shooting mechanics and a focus on exploration, Survive offers an adrenaline-pumping adventure for those who love action and survival games.

Key Features

  • Open-world environment with vast, dynamic jungles to explore.
  • Realistic shooting mechanics for both wildlife hunting and self-defense.
  • Hunting system with various wild animals and different strategies to use.
  • Crafting and resource management to survive in harsh jungle conditions.
  • Day-night cycle and weather effects that impact gameplay.
  • Challenging missions and objectives to complete as you progress.
  • Multiple weapons and upgrades to enhance combat and survival tactics.
  • Immersive graphics and realistic sound effects for a true survival experience.

Project Duration

2 month (June 2024 - August 2024)
